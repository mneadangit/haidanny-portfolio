Featured Project

AI Pet Feeder
with Vision Confirmation

A fully autonomous feeding system that doesn't just schedule meals — it sees, thinks, and confirms.

🐈‍⬛

The Problem

Standard smart feeders are time-based and blind. They dispense food even when the pet isn't there, overfeed, double-feed, or completely miss the pet's presence. There's no intelligence — just a timer.

🤖

The Solution

Motion triggers a camera snapshot. Google Gemini AI analyzes the image to confirm it's your pet. Only then does the feeder dispense the right portion — then a second AI call confirms the kibble actually dropped. Full loop, zero guessing.

How It Works

📡
Step 1
Motion Detected
Tapo camera cell motion sensor triggers the HA automation
📸
Step 2
Snapshot + AI Analysis
Camera takes a snapshot. Gemini Vision confirms: is it the right pet?
🍽️
Step 3
Smart Dispensing
Correct gram portion dispensed based on time-of-day schedule and daily limit
✅
Step 4
Visual Confirmation
Second AI call confirms kibble is in the bowl. You get a push notification.

What's included in the blueprint

✓Anti-double-feeding cooldown (configurable, e.g. 130 min)
✓Daily gram limit tracking — auto-stops when limit is reached
✓Morning double portions, daytime single portions (time-based logic)
✓Customizable AI detection prompt — works for any pet type
✓Smart retry window after false detection (no spam triggers)
✓Single notification per false alarm (failure tracker toggle)
✓Works with any Wi-Fi feeder exposed as a number entity in HA
✓Full Home Assistant Blueprint — 100% configurable via UI, no YAML editing
